Interactive WebGL Demo

Lighting, Shading and Atmosphere

Mini 3D diorama for comparing Gouraud, Phong and Blinn-Phong shading with spotlight, fog and toon rendering. The scene includes an orbital camera and axis-based movement.

Controls

  • 1Gouraud shading
  • 2Phong shading
  • 3Blinn-Phong shading
  • 4Spotlight on / off
  • 5Fog on / off
  • 6Toon rendering on / off
  • PPerspective / orthographic projection
  • DragOrbit camera
  • WheelZoom camera
  • WASDMove camera on X/Z axes
  • Q / EMove camera up / down
  • ShiftFaster camera movement

The Phong and Blinn-Phong specular values are intentionally exaggerated so the visual difference is easier to read in a portfolio demo.
