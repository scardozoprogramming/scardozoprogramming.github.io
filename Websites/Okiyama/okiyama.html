Scope

What I Built

  • Landing and brand pages Home, company information and product-focused sections for a healthcare-oriented brand.
  • Online shop structure Product cards, product detail pages, price display, calls to action and a direct WhatsApp customer-service path.
  • Cart and checkout preparation Local cart storage, quantity controls, totals, cost and discount calculation and form structure prepared for payment-button integration.
  • Compliance and trust pages Legal/privacy pages, cookie banner and clear information architecture for user confidence.

Case Study

Problem, Solution, Result

  • Problem Okiyama already had a website, but the experience needed clearer visual structure, stronger brand consistency and a better purchase path. The previous version did not include payment-button integration, cost and discount calculation, a complete cart flow or direct customer-service support through WhatsApp.
  • Solution I worked on the maintenance and redesign by aligning the interface with the company color palette, simplifying sections that were previously split across too many tabs, adding the cart flow, preparing the payment-button integration, building cost and discount logic and connecting customer support through WhatsApp.
  • Result The improved version is clearer, more organized and easier to navigate, with a simplified structure, stronger visual consistency and purchasing features prepared before the official publication of the maintenance update.
