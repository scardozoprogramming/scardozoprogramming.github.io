  • Landing and brand pages Home, company information and product-focused sections for a healthcare-oriented brand.
  • Online shop structure Product cards, product detail pages, price display, calls to action and WhatsApp contact path.

Case Study

Problem, Solution, Result

  • Problem Okiyama needed a clearer digital presence for presenting products and guiding users toward purchase or contact.
  • Solution I built a responsive website with product information, shop pages, a cart flow and front-end interaction logic.
  • Result The project became a structured web experience prepared for browsing, product comparison and checkout integration.

Responsive Navigation

Mobile menu behavior, sticky header states and clear access to home, company pages, shop and cart.

Product & Shop Flow

Product cards, detail pages, prices, add-to-cart actions and a cart summary prepared for checkout.

Front-End Logic

JavaScript for cart storage, quantity updates, contact form feedback, cookie banner and UI state.

Cart State

Cart data is stored with localStorage so products and quantities persist across the browsing flow.

Checkout Preparation

The payment section is structured for Openpay integration while keeping the current form behavior transparent.

Accessible UI Patterns

Skip links, semantic sections, ARIA labels and readable button states support a clearer user experience.
